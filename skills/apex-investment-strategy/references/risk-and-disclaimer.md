# Risk And Disclaimer Notes

Always keep the public skill in research mode.

## Required Statements

- This is not personalized financial advice.
- Backtests are not live performance.
- Sample data is only for smoke testing.
- Users are responsible for data quality and decisions.
- The tool does not connect to broker APIs or place trades.

## Risk Checklist

Before interpreting any signal, check:

- Does each asset have enough history?
- Are prices adjusted or unadjusted, and is the choice consistent?
- Are dates aligned across markets?
- Are stale or missing rows visible?
- Are fees, spreads, taxes, and liquidity ignored?
- Is the wallet paper-only or real?
- Is the user asking for explanation or for a personal decision?

If the user asks for a personal decision, explain the evidence and caveats instead of deciding.
